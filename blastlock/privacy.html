Privacy Policy

Matthew Productions built the game Blastlock as a commercial application. This service is provided as is and intended for entertainment purposes.

Advertising & Tracking

We use Google AdMob to display ads. On iOS, you may be asked for permission to allow tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. This is to provide you with more relevant ads.

The prompt says: "Allow Blastlock to track your activity across apps and websites to provide personalized game recommendations and improve our analytics. Your data remains anonymous and never shared."

If you choose “Ask App Not to Track”, only non-personalized ads will be shown.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous data to help us improve the game. This includes:

  • Game progress (levels, scores)
  • Feature usage and button taps
  • Session length and crashes

No personally identifiable information is collected or stored.

Data Storage

Your game progress and settings are saved locally on your device. On Apple devices, data may also be synced to iCloud if enabled in your system settings.

Children’s Privacy

This game is not directed toward children under the age of 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

Security

We do not collect personal data. All analytics and advertising data are managed securely through Firebase and Google AdMob in compliance with their privacy practices.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
