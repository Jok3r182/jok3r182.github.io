Privacy Policy

Matthew Productions built the game Blastlock as a commercial mobile app. This service is provided by Matthew Productions and is intended for use as-is.

Information We Collect

Blastlock uses third-party services to improve gameplay experience and support monetization through ads and analytics. These services may collect non-personally identifiable information including:

  • Device type and OS version
  • Gameplay events (e.g. levels completed, time spent)
  • Crash logs and performance metrics
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g. IDFA or GAID)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Firebase Analytics (Google)

Firebase helps us understand how users interact with the game. It collects anonymous usage data only and does not collect personally identifiable information. Learn more: https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics

Google AdMob

AdMob is used to show ads within the app. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to deliver personalized ads. You can opt out of ad personalization through your device settings. Learn more: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

In compliance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, Blastlock may request your permission to track activity across other apps and websites to show personalized game recommendations and improve analytics.

If you decline, we will limit data collection to non-personalized analytics and ads only.

Children’s Privacy

This game is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at blastlock25@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations under the GDPR where applicable.

Data Storage

Game progress and user settings may be stored locally on your device, or synced with Apple iCloud if enabled. Analytics and ad-related data are stored securely by our third-party providers.

Security

We value your trust. While no system is 100% secure, we follow industry practices to protect any data that may be collected.

Your Choices

  • You can limit ad tracking from your device’s settings (e.g. “Limit Ad Tracking” on iOS).
  • You can reset your advertising ID in your system settings.
  • You can opt out of tracking when prompted via the ATT request on iOS.
