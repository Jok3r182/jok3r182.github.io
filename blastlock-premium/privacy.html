Privacy Policy

Matthew Productions built the Blastlock app as a premium commercial game application. This service is provided by Matthew Productions and is intended for use as is.

Data Collection

We use Firebase Analytics to collect non-personally identifiable usage data to understand how players interact with the game. This includes:

  • Anonymous event data: screen views, level completions, and in-game actions.
  • User engagement metrics: session length, retention, crash reports, and performance data.
  • Device identifiers: Firebase may collect device-level identifiers (such as iOS IDFA or IDFV) for analytics.

This data is stored securely by Firebase in accordance with Google’s privacy and security policies. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored by us.

Children's Privacy

Blastlock is rated 9+ and may be accessible to users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Analytics data collected is anonymized and used strictly for app performance and improvement.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Data Storage

Game progress and settings may be stored locally on your device, or synced to Apple iCloud to allow you to restore your progress across devices.

User Rights & Choices

You can choose to limit analytics tracking by:

  • Disabling analytics collection in your device settings (e.g., “Limit Ad Tracking” or Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt if applicable).
  • Resetting your device’s advertising ID in system settings.
  • Uninstalling the app at any time to stop all data collection.

Security

We value your trust in using our app. No personal data is collected or transmitted to any external parties beyond Firebase Analytics.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in the future to reflect app changes or legal requirements. Please check this page periodically for updates.
